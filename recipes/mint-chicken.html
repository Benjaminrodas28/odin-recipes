Mint-Chicken

chicken breast cut into bite-size then stir-fry with fresh lemon juice and mint leaves

A favorite family recipe that my Mom has made for hundreds of guests. Fresh lemon juice and mint leaves are a must! Serve over hot cooked white rice.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless,boneless chicken breast halves-cut into bite-size pieces
  • 1 clove garlic, crushed
  • 1/2 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup margarine
  • 1/4 cup fresh lemon juice
  • 1/2 cup chopped fresh mint leaves

Steps

    Steps1

  1. In a medium bowl,mix together the chicken pieces and garlic. Toss with the flour just to coat, and shake off excess.

    2. Steps 2

  2. Heat the margarine in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken pieces;cook and stir until browned. Add the mint leaves and lemon juice, over and steam for about 5 minute,just until the chicken is cooked through. Do not over cook the chicken or it will be tough.
